    <p>&emsp;&emsp;Chunbo Lin is a Ph.D. student in Electronic Science and Technology at Westlake University. He serves as the Executive President of the University Graduate Student Union, President of the School of Engineering Graduate Student Union, and Director of the Zhejiang Provincial College Astronomy Alliance. From 2024 to 2025, he conducted research as a state-sponsored exchange student in a Nobel Laureate's research group at the Department of Physics, University of California, Berkeley. In 2025, he received his Bachelor of Science degree from the College of Physics (Jianxing Honors College) at Zhejiang University of Technology. Subsequently, he joined Westlake University under the supervision of Professor Min Qiu. He was selected for the National Scholarship Top 100 Outstanding Students Roster by the Ministry of Education and People's Daily, won the top prize "CNKI Scholar Award" in the Contemporary Undergraduate Mathematical Contest in Modeling, received the "Special Recommendation" for the National Scholarship, and was awarded the honorary title of Outstanding Science Popularization Educator for his volunteer work in Yajiang (Garze Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture).</p>
